Meet our team

Laurel Freidenberg

Laurel is a second-year in the college majoring in economics and minoring in Spanish. She began with EnvisionDo during the planning process of the ChicagoGastro conference last year, and loved being a part of this. Laurel aspires to combine her passion for the food industry and her love of startups to eventually work with startups that focus on the food industry and social justice issues related to food. She recently interned with a farm-to-fork startup based out of Columbus, Ohio called Azoti, helping them to expand the reach of their services. She also recently interned with the Ohio Treasurer of State’s office, implementing a new computer program to enhance the efficiency and accuracy of account analysis. Laurel is also fundraising chair for University Ballet, and dances with the group, continuing to pursue her life long love of ballet.

Alice Chang

Alice is a second-year studying Economics and Computer Science. As part of the EnvisionDo team, she works to bring in speakers and publicize events. Alice spent this past summer in Beijing interning for The Paulson Institute, where she worked to strengthen Sino-U.S. relations at the crossroads of economic growth and sustainability. In her spare time, she enjoys going on trail rides, creative writing, and scaring herself through horror movies.
